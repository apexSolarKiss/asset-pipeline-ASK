# Placeholder Furniture Workflow Packet Example

This is a single provisional workflow packet example for one SKU-driven furniture item inside the existing placeholder brand context.

## Product Context

- SKU: `NH-SF-214-OAT`
- product class: sofa
- silhouette: three-seat upholstered sofa with straight track arms
- finish and material facts: oat-colored woven upholstery, matte oak block feet
- fidelity-critical facts: seat depth, arm thickness, seam placement, and leg stance must remain faithful to product references

## Brand And Style Context

This packet assumes the Northline House placeholder brand premise: calm, architectural product presentation for ecommerce and catalog use. Style direction should stay bright, restrained, material-led, and quiet rather than editorial, decorative, or narrative-heavy.

## Bounded Creative Discretion

Creative choice is allowed in how the scene is simplified, how lighting is balanced, and whether the hero view reads more front-three-quarter or slightly more frontal. Those choices must stay inside the brand's restrained visual direction and must not alter product identity, proportions, material behavior, or merchandising clarity.

## Constraints

- category-common constraints: maintain clear product legibility, credible proportions, quiet environments, restrained props, and technically usable governed outputs
- product-class-specific constraints: preserve sofa scale, seat depth, arm profile, and overall massing; avoid camera choices that flatten or exaggerate the body of the piece
- SKU-specific facts or exceptions: keep the oat upholstery neutral and accurate, preserve the matte oak feet, and do not obscure seam placement that helps identify this SKU correctly

## Required Output Set

- governed outputs: one front-three-quarter hero and one side or angled view suitable for controlled ecommerce or catalog use
- review artifacts: one contact-sheet-style review artifact is needed for this packet

## Requested Production Objective

Produce a small governed image set for `NH-SF-214-OAT` that presents the sofa as brand-consistent, product-faithful, and ready to advance through the current verification chain toward release-readiness review.

## What This Packet Is Not

- Netsuite or Airtable integration
- prompt libraries or prompt templates
- runtime orchestration
- schema formalization
- model-routing logic
- UI design
- reusable packet standards
