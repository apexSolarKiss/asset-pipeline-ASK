# Workflow Modes Note

These are current working workflow modes for the repo, used to distinguish different governing logics rather than to impose a final industry taxonomy.

## SKU-Driven Product Imagery

This mode is governed primarily by SKU truth, standardized asset requirements, repeatability, and product clarity. The main pressure comes from keeping product facts stable while producing reliable, comparable outputs across many items.

## Collection / Merchandising

This mode is governed by how products belong together in assortment, collection, or room and set context rather than by a single SKU alone. The main pressure comes from preserving product truth while also making grouping, adjacency, and merchandising relationships legible.

## Marketing / Message-Driven

This mode is governed by a specific message, launch, seasonal need, offer, or channel objective. The main pressure comes from expressing a targeted communication purpose without losing control of what the asset is supposed to say or do.

## Brand Campaign / Editorial

This mode is governed by broader brand world, storytelling, aspiration, and higher creative discretion. The main pressure comes from holding brand coherence and expressive intent together without collapsing into unconstrained image-making.

## Working Rule

These are working workflow buckets for the repo and may be sharpened later, but they should already help distinguish where product truth, collection logic, message intent, and campaign vision exert different kinds of pressure on the workflow.
